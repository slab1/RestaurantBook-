About Chef Amara

Chef Amara Okafor is a passionate culinary artist with over 8 years of experience in creating exceptional Nigerian and West African cuisine. Born and raised in Lagos, she brings authentic flavors and modern techniques to traditional dishes that hold deep cultural significance.


Her culinary journey began in her grandmother's kitchen, where she learned the art of Nigerian cooking from generations of family recipes. She later formal her training at the prestigious Culinary Institute of Nigeria and has since catered over 500 events, from intimate family gatherings to large corporate functions.


Chef Amara is renowned for her mastery of jollof rice, perfectly spiced suya, and traditional soups like egusi and ogbono. She seamlessly blends traditional cooking methods with modern presentation, creating dishes that are both authentic and visually stunning.
