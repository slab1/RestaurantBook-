## Overview

Our loyalty program features five distinct tiers, each offering increasingly valuable benefits. Tiers are based on a combination of loyalty points earned and total lifetime spending, ensuring fairness and rewarding both frequent visitors and high-value customers.

## Bronze Tier

### Entry Requirements
- **Points**: 0+ loyalty points
- **Total Spent**: ₦0+ lifetime

### Benefits

#### Discounts
- **Base Discount**: 0% on all orders
- **Earning Rate**: 1.0x points (2 points per ₦1 spent)

#### Free Delivery
- **Threshold**: No free delivery
- **Delivery Fee**: Standard rates apply

#### Booking Benefits
- Standard booking privileges
- Waitlist access
- No priority booking

#### Special Rewards
- Welcome bonus: 100 points on first booking
- Festival promotions: Standard rates

#### Birthday Benefits
- Birthday message
- 0 bonus points

### Example Bronze Benefits
```
Order: ₦10,000
- Discount: ₦0
- Points Earned: 200 (₦10,000 × 2 pts × 1.0x)
- Delivery: ₦1,500
- Total: ₦11,500 + 200 points
```

## Tier Maintenance

### Maintaining Your Tier

#### Bronze → Silver
- **Requirement**: Earn 1,000 points OR spend ₦50,000
- **Maintenance**: No maintenance needed once achieved

#### Silver → Gold
- **Requirement**: Earn 3,000 points OR spend ₦150,000
- **Maintenance**: Must earn 500 points OR spend ₦50,000 per year

#### Gold → Platinum
- **Requirement**: Earn 5,000 points OR spend ₦300,000
- **Maintenance**: Must earn 750 points OR spend ₦75,000 per year

#### Platinum → Diamond
- **Requirement**: Earn 10,000 points OR spend ₦600,000
- **Maintenance**: Must earn 1,000 points OR spend ₦100,000 per year

### Tier Grace Period
- **Gold**: 90 days grace period
- **Platinum**: 120 days grace period
- **Diamond**: 180 days grace period

During grace period:
- Keep current tier benefits
- Opportunity to re-qualify
- Notifications sent monthly

### Tier Downgrade Protection
- **Platinum/Diamond**: Can buy tier protection for ₦25,000/₦50,000
- **Special occasions**: Automatic protection during major life events
- **Medical emergencies**: Temporary tier freeze available

## How to Progress Between Tiers

### Quick Tier Advancement Strategies

#### Bronze → Silver (1,000 points)
1. **Book 5-10 regular meals** (₦10,000 each = 200 points each)
2. **Write 3 reviews** (100 points each = 300 points)
3. **Refer 2 friends** (500 points each = 1,000 points)
4. **Social media sharing** (50 points × 10 shares = 500 points)

#### Silver → Gold (3,000 points)
1. **Double your dining frequency** (Silver = 1.2x points)
2. **Host group events** (Group bookings = bonus points)
3. **Attend special events** (Event attendance = points)
4. **Partner purchases** (10% partner discounts)

#### Gold → Platinum (5,000 points)
1. **Monthly dining** (₦25,000 × 1.3x = 650 points/month)
2. **Use all double points days** (+50% bonus)
3. **Special occasion bookings** (Birthdays, anniversaries = bonus)
4. **Corporate partnerships** (Business dining = higher rates)

#### Platinum → Diamond (10,000 points)
1. **Weekly dining** (₦40,000 × 1.5x = 1,200 points/week)
2. **Host events** (Large parties = bonus multipliers)
3. **Premium experiences** (Chef's table, wine tastings = points)
4. **Refer high-value customers** (500-1,000 points per referral)

### Bonus Point Opportunities

#### Limited Time Promotions
- **New Menu Launches**: 2x points
- **Holiday Seasons**: 1.5x points
- **Weekend Specials**: 2x points
- **Weather Promotions**: "Rainy day" discounts + bonus points

#### Challenge Programs
- **Monthly Challenges**: Visit 4 restaurants = 1,000 bonus points
- **Seasonal Challenges**: Try 10 seasonal items = 2,000 bonus points
- **Social Challenges**: Share 20 experiences = 1,000 bonus points

#### Collaborative Opportunities
- **Corporate Programs**: Business dining accounts
- **Wedding/Celebration Planning**: Event hosting benefits
- **Travel Partnerships**: Hotel + dining packages

### Investment vs. Frequency Strategy

#### High-Frequency Strategy
- **Best for**: Regular diners, local residents
- **Approach**: More visits, smaller amounts
- **Example**: 20 visits × ₦5,000 = 100,000 spent = Gold tier

#### High-Value Strategy
- **Best for**: Special occasion diners, business leaders
- **Approach**: Fewer visits, larger amounts
- **Example**: 5 visits × ₦60,000 = 300,000 spent = Platinum tier

#### Balanced Strategy
- **Best for**: Most customers
- **Approach**: Mix of frequency and value
- **Example**: 12 visits × ₦25,000 = 300,000 spent = Platinum tier
